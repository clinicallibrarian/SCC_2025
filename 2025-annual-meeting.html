Things are Rocking and Rolling as we prep for the 2025 conference in Little Rock. This year’s theme is: Rock & Roll: Amplify Personal Wellness for Professional Excellence.

The Little Rock LAC is deep into planning a conference where you can share your projects and research, learn new things and network with your colleagues — but we are also adding another layer. We will be taking moments to focus on ourselves… our health. To function at our highest level of professionalism and expertise, our minds and bodies must be functioning as well as possible. Our hope is that everyone will leave the conference informed and rejuvenated.
